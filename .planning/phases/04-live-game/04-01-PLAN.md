## Phase Goal

**As a** member of a subreddit growing its cosmos, **I want to** have the overnight freeze score
my community's day against its goal and stamp the verdict permanently on the ring, **so that** the
daily loop has real stakes and every viewer sees the same achieved/missed result.

(This plan delivers the scoring spine of that story; later plans add live nudging, the realtime
channel, the reveal post, and the reward glyph.)
