<objective>
The retention-hook slice: at the overnight tick, after the ring freezes and scores (plan 01), create
exactly one pinned interactive reveal post that renders the just-frozen universe with a goal/achieved
overlay; and paint a deterministic, permanent reward glyph on any ring whose goal was achieved. Then
verify cross-client determinism — a second client after the tick renders an identical frozen ring +
glyph. After this plan the full daily loop is closed: fill → steer → freeze → score → reveal → reward.

Purpose: LIVE-02 (pinned "what your universe became overnight" reveal post within ~1 min of the tick),
GAME-04 (persistent reward on an achieved ring, visible permanently), and LIVE-03 extended to the
reward + outcome (identical render on every client). Resolves OQ2 (exactly-once reveal guard).

Output: `createRevealPost` in `post.ts`, the reveal-creation + revealDone guard in `runTick`, the
paint-only reward glyph in `paint.ts`/`PhaserPainter.ts`, the per-shell goalAchieved wiring in
`game.ts`, and a blocking cross-client determinism checkpoint.
</objective>

overnight and whether we hit the goal — with achieved days carrying a permanent glow, **so that** the
daily freeze is a shareable event with lasting stakes.
