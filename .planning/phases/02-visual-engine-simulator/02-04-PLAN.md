**As a** player exploring the cosmos, **I want to** scrub through time, zoom, and click a shell to focus it while an always-visible readout tells me that ring's date/stats/goal, **so that** I can read the community's history legibly and navigate the universe.

Make the universe navigable and legible. </objective>
